SELF-BALANCING BOT

Self-balancing bot or the so called inverted pendulum balances itself and also corrects its position automatically on disturbance. The robot actually finds it easier to stabilize if the frame is very tall with a lot of weight on top, instead of a small frame with a low centre of gravity. The physics for this robot is simple, the robot stand in two points lined, the wheel, and it tends to fall and lose his verticality, the movement of the wheel in the direction of the falling rises the robot to recover the vertical position. Self-balancing scooter, two wheels are used so more efficient and can be used for tracking where large bots cannot go, light sensors can be used in the bot and can make it to follow dark line which can be used in dark areas or places where mankind cannot go.
